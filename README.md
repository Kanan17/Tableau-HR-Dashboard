# Tableau-HR-Dashboard

## Business Problem
Employee attrition increases hiring and training costs.
The objective is to identify key factors contributing to employee turnover.

## Dataset
HR dataset containing employee demographics, job satisfaction, department, and attrition status.

## Key Questions
- Which employee segments show the highest attrition?
- How does job satisfaction impact attrition?
- Which departments require immediate HR intervention?

## Key Insights
- Employees aged 26â€“35 show the highest attrition.
- Low job satisfaction is a strong indicator of churn.
- Sales department contributes disproportionately to total attrition.

## Recommendations
- Focus retention programs on early-career employees.
- Improve engagement initiatives in Sales.
- Monitor job satisfaction scores proactively.

## Limitations
- Dataset is static and lacks time dimension.
- External factors (salary, manager quality) are not included.
